Ease of Reports and One Place Management on the Move

Our software allows you to generate reports for all your properties in one place, making it easy to track sales, inventory, and customer data across all locations. You can also manage your properties on the go, with access to your data from any device with an internet connection.

Transfer Products

Our software allows you to transfer products between your properties, ensuring that you always have the inventory you need to serve your customers.

Transfer Service Providers & Staff

With our software, you can easily transfer service providers and staff between your properties. This ensures that your customers can receive the same level of service at any of your locations.

All-Over Access to Client Memberships, Wallets, Gift Cards, Reward Points

Our software provides all-over access to client memberships, wallets, gift cards, and reward points at any of your branches. This means that your customers can use their rewards and memberships at any of your locations, providing a seamless experience and building loyalty.
